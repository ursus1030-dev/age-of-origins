Game events

In-game events explained — what they are, when they run, and what to prioritize.

Alliance events are where we win or lose together. This page gathers what each event is, how points work, and how we play it as a team. For now it covers the Frenzy / Void event — more events and the missing points tables will be added as we pull them from the game.

Frenzy / Void event

Overview

The Frenzy event lasts 7 days and is divided into 5 phase types:

  • Collecting resources
  • Recruiting troops
  • Building and technology levels
  • Kill monsters
  • Killing enemy troops (Kill Phase)

There are 6 possible phase orders, and the order changes every time — you only find out which of the 6 it is once the event starts.

Phase order — the 6 variants

Each variant runs over 7 days and always ends with War (the Kill Phase):

  1. Construction – Troops – Troops – Gather – Zombies – Zombies – War
  2. Construction – Troops – Troops – Zombies – Zombies – Gather – War
  3. Gather – Build – Troops – Troops – Zombies – Zombies – War
  4. Gather – Zombies – Zombies – Construction – Troops – Troops – War
  5. Zombies – Zombies – Construction – Troops – Troops – Gather – War
  6. Zombies – Zombies – Gather – Build – Troops – Troops – War

Points per phase

In each phase, the goal is to meet the requirement to earn rewards. Each task gives points, and there are target tables for each phase.
