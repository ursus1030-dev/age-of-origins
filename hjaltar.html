How officer power works

One explanation for every officer on this page — read it once here, not again on each one.

Level
Raised with EXP items. Bumps her base Attack/HP a little, and feeds into Mastery.
Stars
Raised with officer-specific shards. Each star unlocks a new skill slot and boosts Mastery a lot. The 6th, differently-coloured star opens the Breakthrough skill.
Mastery
Not something you grind directly — it's a combined score from Level + Stars. The higher it is, the stronger every one of her skills gets.
Base skill
Her signature, fixed ability. It's never upgraded with skill books — it only grows stronger through Mastery.
Skill slots
3-4 generic abilities any officer can carry. Levelled with Skill Books, and can be swapped out ('Forget') for a different one. More slots unlock as she gains stars. These are a choice — the ones shown below are just what one player picked.
Breakthrough skill
Her strongest, most unique ability. Upgraded with Fragments (hers plus a generic type), not skill books.
Skin
A cosmetic upgrade — new look and title — that also carries its own small skill bonus.
Equipment
Two separate gear slots (weapon + gear), each with its own enhancement level. A system of its own, on top of everything above.
Power
The single combined score that adds up everything above.
