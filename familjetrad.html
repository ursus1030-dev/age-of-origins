Antiques

Antiques are permanent city-progression items. Each belongs to one of 6 sets — collecting a full set unlocks a bonus set-effect on top of the item's own stat boost. All 52 antiques tracked below, cross-checked against in-game data.

Biochemical Charge

Increases Biochemical troops' Attack/HP/Defense by up to 4% (scales with upgrade level). +20% cross-nation.

Giant Weapons

First 20 seconds of battle: increases Source Spirit, Empress and Warplane Attack by up to 2%.

Strong Will

Reduces damage taken by Melee troops by up to 0.8% and reduces enemy Long/Mid-Range Attack by up to 2.6%.

Rocket Turret

Summons a Rocket Turret in Commander battles — attacks the largest enemy troop group every 2 seconds.

Nano Swarm

Increases all troops' HP/Defense by up to 1.5%. In Commander battles, deals extra random damage to enemy troops.

Brilliant Things

Increases all troops' Attack/HP/Defense by up to 1.2%.
